My Slambook!

A slam book is a notebook passed among friends, typically among children and teenagers, to collect personal information and opinions by answering pre-written questions or prompts. Participants answer questions about their likes, dislikes, dreams, hobbies, and other personal details, often decorating their pages with doodles or photos. Slam books serve as a way to strengthen friendships, learn more about one another, and create a lasting record of memories and shared experiences.
